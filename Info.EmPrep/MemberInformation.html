Member Information Info

The Member Information Form is used to store information into and update information in the Member Database. The database will be sharable with others in the neighborhood and will to be used during emergencies, such as, earthquakes, evacuations, and wildfires. With this form you can store images of

  • yourself,
  • your house mates -- people and pets you live with,
  • your living structure -- view of front entrance.
