Member Information Info

The Member Information Form stores and updates information in the Northside EmPrep Member Database. This database is shared among members of the EmPrep group and is intended to be used during emergencies, such as, earthquakes, evacuations, and wildfires. Also, the database will be useful for neighborhood security purposes.

With this form you can store images of
  • yourself -- selfie,
  • your housemates -- people and pets you live with,
  • your living structure -- view of front entrance so we can locate you by your address
The following files available for download:
  • The current version of the EmPrep Member Database in a comma-separate-variable (.csv) spreadsheet form
Additional files to download can be requested to be put on this list.
