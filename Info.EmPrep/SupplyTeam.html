Supply Team Info

The Supply Team delivers needed supplies (ladders, cribbing, fire suppression equipment, litters, etc. )to Response Teams.They may need to locate and acquire the equipment which is not available in the ICS inventory (e.g., batteries, porto-potties, food, forms, etc.).

  • Takes direction from and reports to the Equipment Manager.
  • Locates needed supplies (in inventory or elsewhere) and delivers them to the location needed.
  • Logs changes in inventory and/or the availability of neighborhood resources (e.g., ladders) with Inventory Manager.
