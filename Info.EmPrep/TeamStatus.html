Team Status Form Info

This form lists all Response Teams, their assignment location, and the personnel composition. Selecting a Response Team takes you to a Change: Response Team Form where you can

  • Change the team's location assignment through the Address Selection Menu.
  • Remove personnel
  • or Add personnel through the Personnel Selection Menu
  • Delete the team altogether.
Selecting an address takes you to its Damage Assessment Form. Selecting a person takes you to their Personnel Information Form. Messages are sent to everyone concerned in the case of Personnel Changes.
